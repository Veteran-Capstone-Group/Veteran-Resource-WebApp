Personas For Veteran Assistance App

Persona 1 - Family or friend

Quentin Romero

Personal Background

Quentin Romero is a 25 year old Caucasian man from the South Valley in Albuquerque New Mexico. He has an uncle who recently left the Army and is having a hard time reentering society. Quentin has little formal education, he stopped going to school at 14 to work for his father's residential construction business. He is unfamiliar with local resources for veterans and is browsing online for something he or his uncle could use to make his uncle's reentry successful.

Technology

Quentin is using a seven-year-old library computer running Windows XP. He is using Internet Explorer.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Quentin is a strong advocate for hard work and determination. He is frustrated that his uncle can't seem to make it after serving his country for most of his life. Quentin values family very heavily and feels responsible for his family's success. Quentin does not use computers very often, mostly limiting his interaction to google maps and youtube videos.

Frustrations and Needs

Quentin is frustrated by the unorganized way veteren resources seem to be distributed. He needs a place that he can direct his uncle to that will help his uncle find friends, get a job, and get help with the day to day. He knows that these resources exist, he is just not sure how to get his uncle in touch with them.

Goals

Quentin wants to connect his uncle with the resources his uncle needs to make it.

User Story

Inexperienced user uses site to find resources.

Sarah Thomas

Personal Background

Sarah Thomas is an African American veteran and a leader in her community. She credits a lot of the success she has had to her military background and the strength of character it left her with. She owns a local technology firm and is frustrated by the lack of representation of veterans in the tech space in her community. She has started a scholarship program that gives five veterans a year free tuition to a local coding bootcamp. She is looking for a way to get the word out to the community she is trying to help.

Technology

Sarah uses an iPad to browse the web from a local coffeehouse. She uses Safari as her browser. She is very familiar with technology and web applications.

Frustrations and Needs

Sarah is frustrated by the lack of areas where she can reach her targeted demographic. She needs a way to reach veterans with the opportunity she wants to provide.

Goals

Sarah wants to put more veterans into the technology sector. She hopes to make the change from combat to coding a more natural and normal transition.

User Story

As a user, I want to submit a resource for others to utilize.
